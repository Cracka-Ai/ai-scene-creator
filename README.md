## 🚀 What is AI Scene Creator?

**AI Scene Creator** is an **AI-powered web app** that generates cinematic scenes and stunning visuals from simple prompts. Create realistic environments, story visuals, and creative content instantly using modern AI models — no signup, no cost, unlimited use.

Whether you need a dramatic Pixar-style 3D render, an intense anime fight sequence, a realistic environment scene, or a multi-scene story with consistent characters — AI Scene Creator has you covered.
